2.2.0 Foundry TDE News (7/30/2021)

Tsaaths Foundry-Channel

Tsaath aka Tom created a whole bunch of new Tutorials in german. Many of those now have english subtitles thanks to Nellkir. If you want to know more on the functions of TDE for Foundry VTT you might want to Check this out.
Also he invested some effort to provide the patchnotes as video. Unfortunately not yet with subtitles. Those might follow later. Changelog

Niemand's Foundry Channel

Niemand also created a channel containing tutorials on foundry and especially The Dark Eye in foundry in german. He is also the first one to create a let's play for foundry and TDE with the adventure "Das Geheimnis des Drachenritters". If you want to learn more find the channel here and have fun.
