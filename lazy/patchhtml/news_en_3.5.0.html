Foundry v10

The next version for Foundry (v10) is already available as a prototype. The dark eye is not compatible with this version. Don't even try to update and just wait until a compatible version has been anounced. Foundry v10 has large underlying changes which will create a lot of work for the migration. If you don't wait you'll see a lot of bugs, error messages and you also might encounter data loss...

Upcoming Contents

Aventurian Herbarium (German)

The module contains comprehensive background information on over 60 fantastic plants of Aventuria, an overview of the basic ingredients and about 70 recipes. In addition, it gives laymen simplified rules for the search for plants. Experienced players can use the focus rules to deepen the herb hunt, individual plant shelf life, and ways to preserve them.

The wild growth generator is also particularly useful. It allows the game master to quickly plants and distribute them on the map as tokens. This provides more variety and more interaction possibilities for the players.

Available soon at the F-Shop.

Aventurian Taverns (German)

The module includes 4 extensively described and elaborated taverns on 6 different maps. The taverns include 4 merchants offering individual services and a total of 15 suggestions for scenarios with different endings. In addition, there are 32 NPCs, including tokens, values and background descriptions.

In addition, the module contains 9 enemies and creatures, some with different stats for different player levels, and as a bonus, a Garadan game board, which contains all the characters as tokens.

Available soon at the F-Shop.

Orks - Hauer & Schwarzer Pelz (German)

Orcs are known mainly as brutal warriors and enemies of men. Few know that they have an an ancient culture in which honor and bravery play an important role.

In the module you will learn more about the background of orcish tribes, gods and culture. It also contains professions, spells, liturgies, animist powers of the featured professions, and orcish professions, as well as orcish weapons and armor. Additionally, it includes as a bonus more than 40 tokens for characters, NPCs, enemies and more. They make creating and playing an orcish or half-orc hero easier. half-orc hero easier.

Available soon at the F-Shop.
